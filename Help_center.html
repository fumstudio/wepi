Step 6:

How to pay ?

To pay for your order, you need to finalize it with one of our agents to make sure everything is correct. You can pay using a bank transfer or an e-wallet. The agent will give you the details. After you pay, please send us proof of payment.

How to track my package ?

To track your package, once your order is verified, the agent will send you the tracking number via WhatsApp. Copy the tracking number and navigate to the website menu. Select 'Track,' paste your tracking number, and click 'Track.' Remember to stay updated with your tracking number, as a new one will be provided every time you place an order.

What is the estimated delivery time for orders?

For free delivery, the shipping time is typically 8-12 business days. If you opt for paid delivery, the shipping time is usually 3-9 business days. Your item will be shipped once your order is verified. The agent will then send you the order number so you can track your package.

Has your package not arrived within the estimated delivery time?

Don't worry, we're here to assist you in locating your package. Please verify that your address is correct and up to date. If you made a mistake, kindly update it. Ensure that your phone is nearby to receive any delivery calls.

Why Returns and exchanges are not supported ?

Personalized items are made just for you. Prior to shipping, you may request pictures from an agent for verification purposes.

Is my package safe ?

We offer safe delivery to all cities across South Africa. All our prints are securely packaged with love and care.
